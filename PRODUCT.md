# Product

## Register
product

## Users
Discerning travelers who want a curated Sri Lanka experience rather than a generic hotel search. They are planning leisure stays, comparing premium properties, and expecting crystal-clear booking confidence and local expertise.

## Product Purpose
Pearlora makes it easy to discover, evaluate, and book extraordinary Sri Lankan stays with the trust of an expert curator. The product exists to turn complex destination planning into a calm, polished journey from search to reservation.

## Brand Personality
Refined. Trustworthy. Warm.
Pearlora feels elegant without being fussy, confident without being cold, and welcoming without being casual.

## Anti-references
Avoid generic OTA noise, bargain-basement flash-sale layouts, and overwhelming price-driven comparison tables. Do not drift toward dark mode, neon gimmicks, or overly technical dashboard-style interfaces.

## Design Principles
- Show premium Sri Lanka travel through calm visual luxury and strong content hierarchy.
- Keep search and booking flows direct, reassuring, and easy to scan.
- Use curated imagery, polished spacing, and subtle brand details instead of loud promotions.
- Preserve trust with clean structure, clear status states, and obvious navigation.
- Make every user interaction feel considered, avoiding clutter and unnecessary complexity.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Aim for WCAG AA-level clarity with readable contrast, labeled controls, and accessible flow states. Support reduced motion and straightforward keyboard navigation, especially for booking reviews and account pages.
